5. iOS-Specific Privacy Features

App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

iOS 14.5 and later requires apps to request permission before tracking users across other apps and websites. When you first launch MakoMadness on iOS, you may see this permission prompt:

"Allow MakoMadness to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites?"

Your Options:

  • Ask App Not to Track: We will only show non-personalized ads (this is our default behavior regardless of your choice)
  • Allow: Helps us optimize ad delivery and may provide slightly better ad experiences

Important: MakoMadness functions identically regardless of your choice. We respect your privacy decision.

You can change your tracking preference anytime:

  • iOS Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Tracking ‚Üí MakoMadness
  • Or: iOS Settings ‚Üí MakoMadness ‚Üí Allow Tracking

SKAdNetwork

We use Apple's SKAdNetwork framework for privacy-preserving ad attribution. This allows us to measure ad effectiveness without collecting personal data.

10. International Users (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data processing
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: "Right to be forgotten" - request data deletion
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

Legal Basis for Processing: We process data based on:

  • Your consent (which you provide by accepting this Privacy Policy)

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: Request information about data collection and use
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of "sale" of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

To exercise your rights, contact us at rrj@agologistics.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.
