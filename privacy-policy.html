MakoMadness Studios, a division of 2N2AK LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you play MakoMadness.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device:

  • Game Progress: Your current level, scores, and game settings are saved locally on your device
  • High Scores: Your best scores are stored to track your achievements

Information Collected Through Third-Party Services:

  • Unity Leaderboards: Anonymous player IDs and high scores for global rankings (no personal information)
  • Unity Analytics: Device type, operating system, game performance data, and crash reports to help us improve the game
  • Unity Ads: Non-personalized advertising data (no tracking of personal information or browsing history)

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

MakoMadness is designed to be safe for players of all ages, including children under 13.

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do NOT require accounts, usernames, email addresses, or names
  • Leaderboards use anonymous identifiers only
  • Ads shown are non-personalized and comply with COPPA requirements
  • We do NOT share children's information with third parties for marketing purposes

Parents: If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at rrj@agologistics.com, and we will delete it.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Save your game progress and settings
  • Display anonymous leaderboards
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs
  • Show non-personalized advertisements
  • Understand how players interact with the game

4. Third-Party Services

MakoMadness uses the following third-party services:

  • Unity Technologies: For leaderboards, analytics, and advertising
    Unity Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google): For non-personalized advertisements
    Google Privacy Policy

These services may collect device information and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We have configured these services to be COPPA-compliant and use non-personalized modes.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information:

  • Game data is stored locally on your device
  • Third-party services use industry-standard encryption
  • We do not store personal information on our servers

6. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Data: You can delete all game data by uninstalling MakoMadness from your device
  • Leaderboard Data: Anonymous scores are retained for leaderboard functionality. To request deletion, contact us at rrj@agologistics.com
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely but contains no personal information

For detailed data deletion instructions, visit: Data Deletion Instructions

  • Opt out of analytics (by contacting us)
  • Refuse consent and stop using the app
