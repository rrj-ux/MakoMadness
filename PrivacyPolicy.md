MakoMadness – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025
Published by MakoMadness Studios, a division of 2N2AK LLC

Thank you for playing MakoMadness (“the Game”).
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile game.

By using the Game, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you play the Game, we and our service providers (including Unity Ads and Unity Analytics) may collect certain data automatically, such as:

Device type, model, operating system

Approximate location (country-level only)

IP address

Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)

Gameplay activity and session length

Crash logs and performance data

Interactions with ads (views, clicks, completions)

This information is collected to improve the Game, prevent fraud, and serve advertisements.

2. Advertising and Analytics

The Game uses Unity Ads and Unity Analytics, provided by Unity Technologies.

Unity may collect data to:

Deliver ads appropriate for your region and device

Limit repetitive ads

Measure ad performance

Detect fraud and misuse

Provide non-personalized or personalized ads (based on consent in applicable regions)

Unity’s privacy policy can be found here:
https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. GDPR (European Users)

If you live in the European Union, you will be asked whether you consent to the use of your data for personalized advertising.

You may choose:

Allow Personalized Ads, or

Use Non-Personalized Ads

Your choice can be changed at any time via the in-game settings screen (if implemented).

4. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

MakoMadness is NOT directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect, store, or process personal information from children under 13.
If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

5. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

Deliver and improve the Game

Provide ads and measure ad performance

Maintain security and prevent fraud

Diagnose crashes and performance issues

Comply with legal requirements

We do not sell personal information.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We may share limited information with the following categories of service providers:

Unity Ads & Unity Analytics (advertising and analytics)

Cloud services used for app distribution (Google Play)

Crash reporting, anti-fraud systems

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for:

Game functionality

Legal compliance

Fraud prevention and security

Advertising and analytics

8. Security
