How to Delete Your Data

Method 1: Complete Data Deletion (Recommended)

‚ö†Ô∏è WARNING: Uninstalling MakoMadness will permanently delete all your game progress, high scores, and settings. This action cannot be undone.

üì± Android Devices (Fire Tablet, Android Phone/Tablet)

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Apps or Applications
  3. Find and tap MakoMadness
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data (deletes all game progress and settings)
  6. Tap Clear Cache (optional, removes temporary files)
  7. Go back and tap Uninstall to completely remove the app

Result: All local data is immediately deleted from your device.

üçé iOS Devices (iPhone/iPad)

Option A: Long Press Method

  1. Long-press the MakoMadness icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Remove App
  3. Select Delete App
  4. Confirm deletion

Option B: Settings Method

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to General ‚Üí iPhone Storage
  3. Find MakoMadness
  4. Tap Delete App
  5. Confirm deletion

Result: All app data is permanently removed from your iOS device.

Method 2: Clear Game Data Without Uninstalling

üì± Android: Clear Data Only

  1. Open device Settings
  2. Go to Apps ‚Üí MakoMadness
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Tap Clear Data

This deletes your game progress but keeps the app installed for a fresh start.

üçé iOS: Reset Game Data

iOS does not allow selective data deletion without uninstalling. To reset your progress:

  1. Uninstall MakoMadness (see Method 1 above)
  2. Reinstall from the App Store
  3. Launch the game for a fresh start

Method 3: Request Deletion of Cloud/Leaderboard Data

If you want to delete your anonymous leaderboard scores or request removal of analytics data collected through third-party services:

  1. Email us at: rrj@agologistics.com
  2. Subject line: "MakoMadness Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your device type (Android/iOS)
    • Device model (e.g., "Fire HD 8", "iPhone 14")
    • Approximate date you installed the game
    • Your approximate high score (to help identify your data)
    • Specific data you want deleted (leaderboards, analytics, all data)

Response Time: We will respond within 7 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

Method 4: Reset Advertising Identifiers

Resetting your advertising ID removes the connection between your device and past ad interactions:

üì± Android: Reset Advertising ID

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Google ‚Üí Ads
  3. Tap Reset advertising ID
  4. Confirm reset

Optional: Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" (though MakoMadness already uses non-personalized ads)

üçé iOS: Limit Ad Tracking and Reset Identifier

iOS 14.5 and later:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Tracking
  4. Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

To reset your advertising identifier:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security ‚Üí Apple Advertising
  3. Tap Reset Advertising Identifier

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?

A: No. Once deleted, your game progress, scores, and settings cannot be recovered. We do not back up player data to our servers.

Q: Will my data be deleted immediately?

A: Local device data is deleted immediately when you clear data or uninstall. Cloud data (leaderboards, analytics) may take up to 30 days to fully remove from all third-party systems.

Q: Does MakoMadness collect personal information?

A: No. MakoMadness does not collect names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, or other personal information. All data collected is anonymous and consists of device identifiers, game progress, and usage analytics.

Q: What if I'm a parent and want to delete my child's data?

A: Follow the same steps above. If you need assistance or have concerns about children's privacy, contact us at rrj@agologistics.com with "Child Data Deletion Request" in the subject line. We will prioritize and respond within 7 business days.

Q: Do I need to delete data from each ad network separately?

A: No. The easiest method is to reset your device's advertising ID (Method 4). This disconnects your device from all ad networks at once. For complete removal, you can also uninstall the app and contact us for leaderboard deletion.

Q: Will deleting data affect my ability to play MakoMadness?

A: If you clear data or uninstall and reinstall, you'll start fresh with a new game. If you only reset your advertising ID, the game continues normally with your current progress intact.

Q: What about GDPR and CCPA rights?

A: You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect (email us)
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (use methods above)
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data (email us, though we collect minimal data)
  • Object: Object to data processing (stop using the app or contact us)

Contact us at rrj@agologistics.com with "Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line.
