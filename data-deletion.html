Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?

A: No. Once deleted, your game progress and scores cannot be recovered. We do not back up player data.

Q: Will my data be deleted immediately?

A: Local device data is deleted immediately. Cloud data (leaderboards) may take up to 30 days to fully remove from all systems.

Q: Does MakoMadness collect personal information?

A: No. MakoMadness does not collect names, emails, addresses, or other personal information. All data is anonymous.

Q: What if I'm a parent and want to delete my child's data?

A: Follow the same steps above. If you need assistance, contact us at rrj@agologistics.com with "Child Data Deletion Request" in the subject line.
